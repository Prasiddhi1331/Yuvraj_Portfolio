ABOUT Me

Yuvraj Singh is a postgraduate computer science student at VIT Bhopal University in India. He is specialising in cyber security. As a co-founder of the university's SIAM Students Chapter, where he heads the technical team, he has proven his leadership abilities. He has shown that he is capable of leading and inspiring a team to success by managing and achieving goals. Yuvraj is a passionate student researcher who is particularly interested in blockchain, metaverse, and quantum computing. His work has been presented at numerous national conferences. Yuvraj has a broad viewpoint and is particularly interested in Indian and global politics and policy-making in addition to his research topics. Yuvraj has a variety of interests outside of academia, including watching the newest Bollywood and Hollywood productions in film and television. He also takes part in sports including cricket, football, Kabaddi, and badminton for pleasure.
